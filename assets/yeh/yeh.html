INTRODUCTION

Tathva '13 beckons all the Young Engineers out there, who love the plier but hate the pen!


Here's your chance to showcase the engineer in you and take home some exciting prizes by just raking up your practical skills.

Exclusively for school students.

The Challenge

This Tathva, test your Engineering quotient with practical hands - on tasks.

Praajna will consist of a series of practical problems for which the participants should figure out and implement a solution with the resources that are provided.
eg: Making a parachute for a boiled egg !

Prelims

The preliminary round is a written round consisting of questions that test the general engineering aptitude of the contestants.

It will be conducted along with Samasya School Quiz at various centres.

Update:
The preliminary round of Praajna is over.
Thank you for your participation. Shortlisted teams will be intimated shortly.

Rules

Frankly, Nothing!

We hate rules. Just come as a team of two and get over with the task given to you.

All materials required will be provided at the venue. No pre-requisites are required.

Everything else will be declared on-spot.

Judging

The judging would be specific to the tasks involved
In general,
  • design of the solution
  • proficiency in fabrication
  • achievement of desired results
will be valued appropriately.

Decisions of the judges shall be final.

    Eligibility

    Following rules contain vital information on eligibility. It is your responsibility to ensure that your images are eligible.

    Eligibility Criteria
    • To be eligible, the Participant's photographic work(s) must be submitted in accordance with the instructions and meet the following guidelines.
    • The photographic work(s) must be in a standard and generally accepted digital photographic format;
    • All photographic work(s) must be the original work of the Participant and the photographic work(s) must not contain material that violates or infringes another's rights, including but not limited to privacy, publicity or intellectual property rights;
    • The photographic work(s) must not contain material that is inappropriate, indecent, obscene, hateful, defamatory, slanderous or libellous;
    • The photographic work(s) must not contain material that promotes bigotry, racism, hatred or harm against any group or individual or promotes discrimination based on race, sex, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation or age;
    • The photographic work(s) must not contain material that is unlawful, in violation of or contrary to the law.

    Image Format

    IMAGE REQUIREMENTS
    1. PREPARATION
    • Images should be of .jpg format.
    • The image should be at least 1000 pixels long or 800 pixels high.
    • The maximum file size for uploaded images is 5 Mb.
    • Up to 3 images can be submitted per person per category.
    2. EDITING AND ADJUSTING YOUR IMAGE
    • Minor digital adjustments may be undertaken in order to optimise an image. Stitched panoramas and HDR (high dynamic range) images are only acceptable where the combined parts are all made around the same time and the integrity of the subject is not compromised.
    • Any alterations to the original photograph not listed here (including the addition or removal of key elements of the image) are not allowed and will render the entry ineligible. Images may not have any watermarks or captions on them.

    Judging

    Theme Based Category
    The ultimate objective is to have a thought provoking and entertaining photography exhibition for viewers online. So 50% weightage is given for the number likes and shares for a photograph and the other 50% is based on the decisions made by jury.
    Based on the photographs submitted to the contest, they will be judged based on the following elements:
    • Does the photo tell a story?
    • Appropriateness of description.
    • Interpretation and the clarity of the theme to the viewer.
    • Creativity and originality of the depicted theme.
    • Quality of composition based on the theme
    General Category
    Here viewers are the sole judges. The winner is decided by the likes and share that one get for his/her photographs.

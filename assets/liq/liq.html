INTRODUCTION


In this present scenario of increasing competition, logic and aptitude are your passport to sublime heights. LogIQ, through Tathva'13, provides an opportunity to hone your skills and prove your mettle against the brightest minds of today. So gear up for the ultimate battle of minds.

Event Specifications

  • LogIQ is a quizzing event which tests basic mathematical and logical skills.
  • A maximum of two participants are allowed per team.
  • The event consists of two preliminary rounds followed by a final round.
  • The first preliminary round is a general written quiz while the second one consists of a set of puzzles.
  • A total of six teams will be selected for the finals.
  • The finals will be a multiple round quiz comprising of audio and visual based questions along with general quiz rounds.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

The participants are required to assemble the given machine parts so as to bring out the required machine component from the given assembled machine drawing.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • All the participants are requested to produce their college ids and Tathva ids before the event co-ordinators.
  • A maximum of three members are allowed per team.
  • Cross college teams are allowed.
  • The event consists of two rounds.
  • The preliminary round will be a written test of a duration of 20 minutes where the participants general concept on machines and their principles will be put to test.
  • The qualified teams shall proceed to machine assembly test which shall also include a viva part to test the basic concepts.
  • The mechanism to be assembled will be informed on the spot.
  • The qualified participants will be provided with the all the necessary components and drawing.
  • Use of any machine part other than that provided will lead to disqualification
  • Any sort of misconduct during the round shall lead to disqualification
  • Decision of judges is final and binding to all.

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • The preliminary round will serve as only a qualification round.The marks obtained will not be carried on for final evaluation.
  • Evaluation will be based on:
Time consumed
Minimal errors made
Basic knowledge on concepts
Correctness of assembly
  • Final marks shall be based on the following format
S = (N - T) + 20n - 10y + 5x
S: Total score
N: Total time given
T: Total time taken
n: Number of components correctly assembled
y: Number of wrong comp assembled
x: Viva questions correctly answered

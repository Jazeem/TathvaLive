INTRODUCTION

The advancements of technology over the past few decades have revolutionized the field of surveying. Many modern instruments and novel techniques are available now. A Total Station is one such instrument-it is an electronic theodolite integrated with an Electronic Distance Meter (EDM).

Workshop Details

The work shop is intended to introduce you to the use and working of Total Station, mainly covering:
  • A Theory Session
  • Hands-on experience in the field: Measurement of angles, co-ordinates, distances etc.
  • A Session on Data Processing: Transferring the data to a computer using application software.
Printed hand-outs (explaining the theory and working) will be issued to every participant.

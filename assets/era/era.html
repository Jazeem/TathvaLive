INTRODUCTION

Are you the digital warrior who gets an adrenaline rush when faced with a problem to sink your teeth into? Does the tick of a clock leave you shaken and stirred? Do you feel right at home in the middle of resistors, transistors, soldering, CROs and capacitors?

Then E RACER is just the event for you!


The machines are rising up against humans. They plan to eliminate all of human kind with one fatal attack.
A nuclear holocaust!



You stand a chance at preventing this disaster. But the clock is ticking and with each passing second they are growing aware of your presence!
DO you have the guts to take up this challenge?


EVENT DETAILS

The event will be conducted in three rounds:

Prelims:

a.The prelims will comprise of a questionnaire related to basic electronics and current events in the e-world.
b. At most 10 teams will be selected to the next round.


The Race is on!

"Wheels have been set in motion, and they have their own pace, to which we are...condemned."
- Tom Stoppard, Rosencrantz and Guildenstern Are Dead

1. To prevent the disaster you need to get to the machines' nuclear facility, the location of which is unknown. En-route you will face many hurdles surviving which you get closer and closer to your destination.
2. The hurdles would comprise of tasks that will be fun, logical and/or relating to electronics
3. Marks will be awarded based on the efficiency with which you have overcome the hurdles.
4. A maximum of 4 teams will get through the race.

Total annihilation:

The countdown has begun. The clock is ticking. The fate of all of human kind lies in your hands.

a. The last round will be a surprise round with one or more stages relating to Circuit Designing, logic design,etc.
b.In case there is no clear winner, the winners will be selected on the discretion of the event managers.


EVENT RULES


1. Each team will consist of 3 members.
2. Spot registrations are permitted.
3. Use of calculators or any electronic devices other than that which are provided, are not allowed.
4. Decision of event managers will be final.

INTRODUCTION



If you can handle yourself on the roads, accelerate at the right moment, cut corners with high speed drifting and think you have what it takes to be the best on road,
put your skills to the test and see if you really have what it takes to be a Top Notch Street Racer!!

Need for Speed: Most Wanted (commonly known as NFS: MW) is a racing video game featuring street racing-oriented game play. Easily among the most popular racing games of all times, this instant classic has had players hooked ever since its release. Though many iterations have come out in the NFS series, NFS: MW remains an all-time favourite because of its competitive nature and gamers have been known to keep practicing to shave off those crucial milli-seconds off their lap times. Being a racing game, it's easy to pick up and play and the adrenaline rush of speeding along and overtaking into the pole position is known to all.

Qualification Round

  • All Tournament participants will play 3 laps and the qualifier for further rounds will be decided by best time basis! (TOP 16)

  • Race course: Dunwich Bay.
  • At the end of each match, players must maintain the final screens and receive confirmation from a referee.
  • Any participant failing to complete the race will be disqualified.

Rules (Common)

  • Game Type: Circuit
  • No. of Laps: 3
  • Courses: Courses will be given at random and will be given on the spot. ( Players will be informed before the tournament of any such modifications)
  • Participants are not allowed to use their own profiles, only Immolation provided save files can be used for car setting.
  • Players will be given 2 minutes to setup the car and its performance before the game.

Rules (Competitive)

  • There will be two levels: level 1 (all 16 qualifiers), and level 2 (top 8 from level 1).
  • Courses: Heritage Heights, Diamond, Switchback, East Park, Omega, Dunwich Bay, Oil Refinery, Bay Bridge and Clubhouse.
  • Competition: All qualifying participants will compete in a "Circuit" mode in groups of 4 on LAN.
  • LEVEL 1: The first two players to finish all 3 laps will qualify to the next round (level 2).
  • LEVEL 2: The player, who finishes first, will qualify for the semi-finals.

Rules (Semi-Finals)

  • Courses: Heritage Heights, Diamond, East Park, Dunwich Bay, Bay Bridge and oil refinery.
  • Competition: All 4 qualifying participants will compete together in 6 races in "Circuit" mode on LAN.
  • Winning a race: The winner of a race is the first player to complete 3 laps of the circuit course and cross the finish line.
  • Qualifying: The 2 players winning at-least 2 races will be qualified for the finals.

Rules (Finals)

  • Courses: Heritage Heights, Diamond, East Park, Dunwich Bay and Bay Bridge.
  • Competition: All qualifying participants will compete 1 vs. 1 in 5 races in "Circuit" mode on LAN.
  • Winning a race: The winner of a race is the first player to complete 3 laps of the circuit course and cross the finish line.
  • Winning the tournament: A player winning 3 races out of 5 will be the "winner" of the tournament.

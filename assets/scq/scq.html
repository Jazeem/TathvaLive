INTRODUCTION

Young school buzzes!!
Samasya awaits you!!

This is your one chance to prove yourselves to be the know-it-alls of our state as you battle it out at Kerala's biggest school quiz, Samasya . Pack your bags and catch your train, because this October NIT Calicut is the place to be.

Rules and Regulations

  • Teams of two members can register at their nearest venue by 9.00 am on 28th September, 2013.
  • The registration fee per team will be Rs. 50
  • Any number of teams may participate from a school.
  • Open for students of classes from VIII to XII.
  • 20 teams will be shortlisted for the finals from all over the state.
  • One team from each centre will be selected for the finals, if they clear the minimum cut off. Else, teams will be selected based on their scores.
  • Registration will be on the spot and participants will be required to produce their school ID.

Age Of Empires II is the sequel to the award-winning computer game, Age of Empires, a real time strategy game in which players are allowed to pick a civilization from 13 unique ones and battle it out with their opponents.

Battle it out with the best gamers of South India at Blitzkrieg!

Specification

  • Registration Fees: Rs. 300/- per team for 3 vs 3

  • Rs.100 per person for 1 vs 1.
  • Competition format: 3 vs 3 AND 1 on 1
  • Match format: Single elimination i.e. Knockout (may be changed to Double Elimination i.e., both teams may be knocked out depending on the minimum score if necessary).
  • Match winner: The first player/team who defeats the opponent(s) in standard conquest rules, or the player/team who's opponent(s) surrender by typing gg/ff/forfeit into the All-Chat.

Rules

General :

  • All teams must report at the venue at the time informed.Teams not reporting within 10 mins of the allotted time, will lead to disqualification. The time will be informed at least 45 minutes in advance.

  • The administrator's decision will be final in case of any dispute.

  • Game will be hosted by Blitzkrieg using referees.

  • Team members are not allowed to change seats once the aoe map has started to load.

  • At the end of the match, competitors must maintain the final screenshots and receive the confirmation from the admin.

  • Bug Exploitation: Exploitation of any known or newly discovered bug during the match will result in immediate disqualification of the team. Excuses will not be entertained. Not knowing the bug is not an excuse, competitors are expected to know the current bugs.

  • Definition of Bugs

    • a. Anything abnormal which will give a competitor / team an unfair advantage
    b. Anything which will cause interruption / fatal error to the game

  • Penalties

    • a. Warning: After a warning is given, the game goes on. If these warnings are accumulated by the team during the match, the team will be disqualified from the tournament.
    b. Game Loss: A 'Game loss' given to a team means that the team will automatically lose the game, irrelevant of the actual match result.
    c. Disqualifications: A team disqualification will lead to the team being removed from the tournament. They will not be allowed to take part in the current event.

  • Saves
    • a. First save will be 15 minutes into the game
    b. Second save onwards will be every 15 minutes of the game.
    c. Admins/Referees have the right to request for additional game save at any moment during the tournament.

  • Any competitor found to have intentionally disconnected from the game will give that competitor's team a loss for that match.

  • If any competitors get disconnected within 15 minutes after the game start, the game will be restarted with same civs on same players.

  • If any competitors get disconnected 15 minutes after the game start, the game will be reloaded from the latest save.

  • In case of game crash, the game will be reloaded from the latest save.

  • Players are not allowed to abuse other players through the chat interface.

  • Pauses are allowed. Before pausing and un-pausing , you need to inform your enemy about this, so that you don't get any advantage. Pauses must be for genuine reasons only.

  • Any use of hacks like maphack or any software or scripts which gives any unfair advantage is banned and will result in instant disqualification from the tournament.

  • Players are required to conduct themselves in a professional manner, showing sportsmanship and respect for everyone involved. Any flaming or insulting will result in team-disqualification.

  • Team or player names may not be vulgar. While it is up to the admins' discretion, as a guideline, avoid the following: profanity, hate-speech, drug use, or sexual references.

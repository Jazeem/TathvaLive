INTRODUCTION

    Tathva has issued a call to the tech hobbyists of the nation, for Prahelika is where all exciting journeys reach their zenith. Tech whizzes from across the country take on each other to prove their worth and for the spectacular prize money. So if you live, think, and breathe technology, this is a quiz not to be missed

    Rules and Regulations

    • Participation is restricted to college students.
    • Registration open to college students only.
    • A team can consist of a maximum of 2 members.
    • Any number of teams from an institution is allowed.
    • Registration for the event will be separate i.e. participants need not register for Tathva.
    • Participants must produce a valid ID card during registration.
    • Those who have registered for Tathva need not register for the event separately.
    • The registration fee is Rs.50 per head, if not registered for Tathva.
    • Cross college teams are allowed.

    Prizes

    First Prize : Rs.5000
    Second Prize : Rs.3000
    Third Prize : Rs.2000

    No of participants

    Min : 1
    Max : 2

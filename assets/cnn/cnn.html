Problem Statement

Create a contraption to guide two balls through two different paths so as to make them fall into a bucket at the same time.

Rules and Regulations

  • A team may consist of a maximum of six members.
  • All teams should register online for participating in this event.
  • The balls should start simultaneously.
  • The distance between the starting point and bucket must not exceed 2.5m.
  • Cross college teams will also be allowed to participate in the event.
  • The team members should produce a valid identity card of their college.
  • All Teams should submit an abstract to the event manager with Subject: Abstract-Contraption (Soft copy/Hard copy) on or before 10thOctober 2013.
    • Abstract should contain following details:
  • Tathva ID No.(Obtained after online registration)
  • Event ID No.
  • Name of Participants.
  • Plan or layout of the contraption.
  • Total number of steps and a short description of each step. Sketches and flowcharts can be made about each step.
  • The various energy conversions involved.
  • All teams must submit abstracts before the above mentioned date in order to get accommodation facility within the campus during the period of TATHVA '13.
  • The contraption must have a minimum of 15 steps in total.
  • A maximum of 1 hr. will be given for the teams to set up their contraptions.
  • The participants can use any kind of ball of diameter less than 75 mm.(Not larger than a tennis ball).
The following will be provided at the venue:
a) Bucket
b) Extension cord.
c) Benches and desks.

Judging Criteria

  • The safety of the contraption must be satisfactory to the judges and the coordinators.
  • If the judges feel that a particular idea is repeated in two or more different steps, the redundant steps won’t be counted among the 15 stipulated steps and points will be reduced.
  • Judge’s decision shall be final and binding on all.
  • Innovative steps will be awarded bonus points and shall be decided by the judge and coordinators.
  • Total score, X = 15S + 25E – 15I – 5T – 10R + Q + J

• S = Total number of steps.
• E = Total number of energy conversion.
• I = Number of interventions.
• T = Extra time taken by the second ball to reach the bucket in SECONDS.
• R = Number of repeating steps.
• Q = Abstract clarity(Out of 50 depending on similarity between abstract and contraption) .
• J = Points for innovative steps (between 10 and 50 depending on the technique involved)

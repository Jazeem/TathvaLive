INTRODUCTION


NIT Calicut presents to you Tathva Rubiks Open,
a speed cubing event, recognized by the
World Cube Association.

Event Specification

  • All participants must register online.
  • Events will be conducted in four categories: 2x2, 3x3, 4x4 and 3x3 one handed.
  • Competitors must bring their own puzzles. Competitors may borrow puzzles from other competitors, although both competitors must be ready to compete when they are called for.
  • Competitors must be present at least 15 minutes before the starting of the event and ready to compete when they are called to compete for a round.
  • Decision made by the organisers will be final.
  • For detailed rules and regulations, visit: http://worldcubeassociation.org/regulations/

INTRODUCTION


For all automobile fanatics, Tathva '13 presents the ultimate car disassembly and vehicle design workshop – AutoInovace. Watch as real time automobiles are dismantled and explore the detailing of the machinery through hands on experience. Acquaint yourself with the mechanics of motorized vehicles and redesign existing models, innovating with every nut and bolt! Venture into the world of automobiles and build your own warehouse of engineering ideas.

Workshop Highlights



  • Live demonstration on Maruti 800.
  • Introduction to advance Vehicle technologies (ABS, TCE, EBD etc.)
  • Introduction to advance engine technologies (MPFI, CRDI, I-VTECH etc.).
  • Introduction to vehicle designing and simulation.
  • Hybrid vehicles & Engine management system.
  • Training material (eBooks) for each participant.
  • Authorized certification.
  • Intern and research opportunities , R&D projects under expert guidance.

Workshop content

  • Demonstration on working of vehicle components and advancement over time.
  • The vehicle will be driven into the venue, dismantled and components explained. At the end of workshop, the vehicle will be re-assembled and its working shown.
  • Advance engines : CRDi,MPFi,ECU as Brain and Algorithm.
  • Vehicle Layout : Vehicle types,layout, Component Placement, Utility Vehicles.
  • Disassembly: Complete disassembly of Commercial car and discussion on disassembled components.
  • Vehicle integration : Interior/Exterior component integration, Trims, ergonomics.
  • Vehicle accessories : HVAC, Instrumentation cluster.
  • Vehicle Safety : ABS,ARS,TCS,Climate control, Air bags, Crash Testing and stability programs.

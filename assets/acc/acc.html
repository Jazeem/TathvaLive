INTRODUCTION



Do the moves and make your automation travel through a fascinating entangler!

PROBLEM STATEMENT


Manipulate a wired/wireless robot controlled solely by hand gestures through a challenging obstacle course in the least possible time.

ARENA

  • The arena would span a total area of 4.5m x 2.5m . The inner width of the track through which the bot has to travel will be 35cm. The whole of the track would be bounded by boulders. The course will offer a number of hurdles and flag points , crossing which, you can earn extra points.
  • There will be two paths, one long, flat path and the other, a shorter bridge inclined at a maximum angle of 30 degrees. The participants are free to decide the path to be taken by their bot.

MACHINE SPECIFICATION

  • The maximum dimension allowed for the bot is 20*20*12 cm. Also, the bot should be no smaller than 6cm * 5cm * 2cm.
  • The machine should be controlled by a wireless/wired mechanism that uses only wrist movement to manipulate the movement of vehicle.
  • The machine cannot be made from lego parts or any readymade assembly kits. Readymade microcontroller -boards, readymade sensors kits and accelerometers may be used.
  • Power supply for the system will not be provided.

CONTROLS


The Machine must be controlled using hand movements only.

EVENT RULES

  • Duration of each match is 8mins.
  • The teams are required to complete the track in minimum possible time.
  • The vehicle should only move through the path specified.
  • If at any point during the race the vehicle encounters a problem, the participants will be given 1 minute to go up to the vehicle and fix it.
  • There will be three matches for each team from the starting point.The best of three will be considered.
  • In case of ties, a re-match will be held.
  • The contestants should be ready with the circuit diagrams, algorithms and code listing (if any) to give proper answers to any questions by the judges.
  • The event managers are reserved the right to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit. Change in rules, if any, will be highlighted on the website and notified to the registered participants.

TEAM SPECIFICATION

  • Each team can have a maximum of 3 members.
  • Team members will have to produce valid ID card of their institute.
  • Cross college teams are permitted.

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • The judge's decision shall be final and binding.
  • Score = 50 - [(your best time-Event best time) / Event best time] * 40 -(No of Red Blocks hit)+ (no. of green flag poles covered).

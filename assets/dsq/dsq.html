INTRODUCTION



Technology, as we know it, gains new dimensions when we incorporate mathematical essence into various realms of science. Spanning across various engineering disciplines, mathematical divinity is presented in its latest incarnation- The Descartes Square.

A voyage into the enthralling world of numbers beckons- Kick start your senses, spruce up those rusty corners of your brain, be ready to fight it out, the power of analyticity and clarity of thought being your only allies.

Event

  1. Descartes Square is a unique, one of its kind event which encourages participation from all engineering branches.
  2. This event tests your speed, logical acumen, imagination, reasoning power and analytical abilities.
  3. The event comprises of two rounds-a preliminary round and a final round.
  4. The prelims consist of a pen-paper round putting to test the above mentioned skills of participants in disciplines of geometry, algebra and ciphers breaking.
  5. Participants have to answer a total of 25 questions in a span of 30minutes, which include 15 multiple choice and 10 fill in the blanks.
    6. Participants who clear the first round and are among the top 8 teams are eligible for the second round. However, the number of teams through to the second round is likely to change during the course of the event depending upon the participation.
  6. The second round will test the participant's surveying skills.

Marking

FIRST ROUND:
  1. Multiple choice questions carry 3 points for each correct response. The pattern regarding the different MCQ's and the expected responses will be specified in the test.
  2. Each correct response in the fill in the blanks section fetches 5 points.
  3. No Negative marks or Partial marks will be awarded for any section.
  4. Judge's decision will be final regarding the answers to the questions in this round.

SECOND ROUND:
  1. The decision regarding the marking scheme is left to the event manager's discretion.

INTRODUCTION



INTERFACE is the best inter college exhibition competition inviting participants from all over the nation. Organised by IEEE NITC Student Branch, the competition provides a platform for budding engineers and innovators. Working models under any category of engineering are welcome. The entries are shortlisted based on the technical and creative content in the project.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Working Models adhering to any field of engineering with a strong touch of innovation are to be submitted.

SPECIFICATIONS

  • A team representing an institute can consist of a maximum of 6 members.
  • Multiple teams can represent an institute.
  • Projects coming under any wing of engineering are welcome.
  • The participating teams must submit an abstract to interface@tathva.org on or before 10th October 2013. The abstract must contain the outline of the projects and other necessary details.
  • 30 teams will be shortlisted based on the abstracts submitted and will be informed.
  • A team can submit a maximum of five different projects/prototypes.
  • The projects will be put up for public display.

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • The evaluation procedure includes two stages, one on each day of the exhibition, done by the distinguished faculty of NIT Calicut.
  • Creativity and extend of innovation are the primary basis of project evaluation with a small weightage for the project presentation. The practical application of the project and its scope will have equal emphasis.
  • Decision of the judges will be final and binding.

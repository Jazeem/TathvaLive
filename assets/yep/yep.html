INTRODUCTION

Scientists investigate that which already is, Engineers create that which has never been.
- Albert Einstein



Unleash the Engineer in this Tathva. Present a splendid project and battle out with best minds of your age. Gear up and let Tathva nurture the engineer in you.

Exclusively for school students.

Problem Statement

Present a project pertaining to any field of science and engineering. Projects adhering to environmental and social issues are strongly encouraged.

Rules

The event will take place in the form of two rounds:
Screening round
  • Participants are required to submit a brief description of their project to youngengineer@tathva.org.
  • Teams can consist of any student from 8th to 12th class.
  • There is no limit on the number of teams from a particular school.
  • Images and videos of the projects are encouraged.
  • Among the projects, a preliminary evaluation will be done by the organizers.
  • Top 40 teams will be selected for the final round.
  • Students should submit their abstracts before 10th of October.
Exhibition
  • The selected teams are to exhibit their projects during Tathva.
  • It will be in the form of a public exhibition wherein the participants would have to describe their projects to the public as well as the judges.
  • The judges would finally evaluate them and select 2 winning projects.
  • Recommendations and feedback from the organizers would be provided for as many projects as possible.

Judging Criteria

The evaluation will take into account the following points:
  • Innovation
  • Benefit to society
  • Simplicity of design
  • Cost of production/implementation
The evaluation pattern pertains to both preliminary as well as final round

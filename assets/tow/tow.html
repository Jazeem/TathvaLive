INTRODUCTION


From basic Linux know-how to advanced shell scripting, it's all here. So grab your thinking hats, and get ready to battle it out in Tux of War. The details about the schedule of the contest will be updated soon.

General Rules

  • Maximum two participants per team.
  • Any number of teams are allowed from a college. However the members of a team must be from the same college.
  • There will be a maximum of three rounds.
  • The first round will be an online qualifying round from which a maximum of 10 teams will be shortlisted.
  • The final round will be conducted at NITC campus during Tathva.
  • The event co-ordinator's decision shall be final.

Rounds

    First Round
  • This will be an online round.
  • Questions will consist of MCQs and one-liners based on UNIX/Linux commands, tools, concepts and philosophy.
  • Duration will be 30 minutes.
  • This will be an elimination round and only the qualifying teams will appear for next 2 rounds.
  • The contest will be held on a separate contest portal.
  • This first round will be held on 10th October between 9 pm and 9 45 pm.
  • An online portal will be opened up on the day just before the scheduled time. The link of this portal will be displayed in this page.
    • Other Rounds
  • Details about the last two rounds will be provided on the contest portal

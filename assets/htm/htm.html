Problem Statement

Here the participants will have to shoot targets of specific color (RED,BLUE or GREEN) which will be given at the time of competition with a laser.

Bot Specifications

  1. The robot should fit in a cubical box of dimensions 30cm*30cm*30cm.
  2. The laser to be used should be a point laser/pencil laser which is easily available in market.
  3. Lego kits and ready-made mechanisms are not allowed.

Arena Specifications



  1. The arena consists of a target board on which colored circular targets will be placed.
  2. The target board is white in color with dimensions 300cm*200cm
  3. The board will contain circular targets of diameter 6cm.
  4. The color of the targets are Red(#FF0000),Green(#008000) and Blue(#0000FF)
  5. There is a limit line 300cm away from the board which the robot should not cross at anytime of the game.

Judging Criteria

Round1
  1. The board will have randomly kept targets of the three colors red, blue and green
  2. The robots will be required to mark multiple targets of a specific color told on the spot with the laser.
  3. The points will be given on the basis of closeness to the target
  4. The robot must mark the target for at least 15 seconds for the organizers to determine the position.
  5. Failure to mark a target or marking targets of different color will lead to a penalty of 10 points.
Round 2
  1. This will be a rapid fire round in which the robots have to mark all the given targets of a specific color in minimum possible time.
  2. The points will be given on the basis of fastest completion of the course.
  3. Penalty of 10 points will be given for each miss or marking of targets of different color.
  4. In this round the robots will have to mark the targets for at least 5 seconds.
Tie Condition
In case of a tie the teams who have the same scores will again compete in round 2 but this time the positions of the marking will also be noted.

Point Distribution

Round 1
  1. The target will have circular regions just like a dart board which will determine the points.
  2. Each target will have a bull's eye at the center which will have 100 points.
  3. The region of 1 cm radius will have 90 points
  4. The region of 2cm radius will have 60 points.
  5. The rest will have 40 points.
Round 2
  • The formula for calculating the point is (1/time in seconds)*1000.
    • Tie Round
  • The formula is Points scored w.r.t closeness of the target + (1/time in seconds)*1000.
    • the team with highest points will win.

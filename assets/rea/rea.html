INTRODUCTION

Self compacting concrete has become an indispensible need for engineers given the constraints normally associated with the congestion of reinforcement, non-availability of skilled labour and difficulty in places of fresh concrete.
Here at tathva’13 we invite you to come up with your own design mix of concrete having a better self compaction in the most economical manner.
A picture depicting the denseness of formwork and the requirement of self compacting concrete

PROBLEM STATEMENT

The sample mix (M40 Grade) submitted by the participating team will be subjected to Self Compacting Concrete tests.

SPECIFICATIONS

1. The participating team is supposed to bring the required materials for the SELF COMPACTING CONCRETE TEST at the laboratory of NIT CALICUT.
2. The participating team is supposed to perform the test in front of the judges.SCC apparatus will be made available to the participating team.
3. The mix prepared must be in agreement with M40 (AS PER IS CODES).
4. The participating team must provide sufficient data about the sample regarding:
• Weight of cement, fine aggregate, coarse aggregate,other materials water used.
• Sieve analysis details,specific gravity of materials used.
5. The event will be strictly conducted on the first day of tathva.
6. Maximum number of candidates per team is 3.
7.The participating team must possess a valid tathva id card.

JUDGING CRITERIA

1. The most appropriate test results -40% weightage.
2.The mix with minimum cement to aggregate ratio (by weight)-30% weightage.
3.The sample details provided by the participating team -10% weightage. 4.The remaining 20% weightage will be given depending on the results obtained from on spot tests.

INTRODUCTION



Wheels is an annual auto-tech show, the largest of its kind in India, conducted at National Institute of Technology Calicut as a part of Tathva, its techno-management festival. With tons of exotic and vintage cars, superbikes, off roaders, vehicle component expo and automobile workshops and talks, it pulls a large crowd from all over the country.

AUTO-TECH SHOW



Wheels focuses not only on the vehicles, but on the technology behind it. Expect a display of engines, performance parts and informative talks too.

VINTAGE



The most sought after collection of vintage cars and motorcycles - dating back even to the 1900s. Learn more about the history and the design aesthetics at its time of making.

EXOTICS



Premium vehicles never fail to turn eyes - with our exclusive display of ultra-modern cars and superbikes, get to know where the present automobile industry stands at.

BIKE STUNT SHOW


Adventurous and thrilling performance by teams of trained professionals - expect the unexpected!

OFFROAD VEHICLES


The world is not flat - see a collection of SUVs, Jeeps and other 4x4 vehicles built just for enduring even the steepest climbs and the longest drives.
